Massage 101

Swedish Massage Deep Tissue Sports Massage Prenatal
Swedish massage is the most common and best-known type of massage. If it’s your first time at the spa or you don’t get a massage very often, Swedish massage is the perfect massage for you. For deeper work and if you can tolerate more pressure, even momentary discomfort, to get relief from muscle pain, it’s better to book a deep tissue massage.
Deep tissu e massage is designed to relieve severe tension in the muscle and the connective tissue. This type of massage focuses on the muscles located below the surface of the top muscles. Deep tissue massage is often recommended for individuals who experience constant pain, are involved in heavy physical activity, and patients who have sustained physical injury.
Sports Massage
The perfect massage for both baby and mommy. This massage is given in a side line position so no extra weight is on your tummy. Prenatal massage helps the aches and pains that may come with pregnancy, and leaves mom in a more relaxed state of mind, which is great for the baby. *Warning Prenatal massage is not recommended during the first trimester because it might increase the risk of miscarriage. Instead, begin receiving your massage in the second trimester. Consult your doctor about the risk of massage therapy if you have certain conditions. For example, massage is not recommended for women at risk of pre-term labor or those with clotting disorders.
